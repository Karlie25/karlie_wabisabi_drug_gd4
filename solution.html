Wabi Sabi provides consultant sessions with professionals before the brain stimulation procedure to understand the ultimate goals of each customer. After consultation sessions, doctors will design operation plans to suit individual wishes. The brain's frontal lobe is where humans form our personality traits, memories, speech and language productions, control feelings, and management attention. To switch to characteristics that customers want, professionals will use electricity to trigger the frontal lobe of the individual's brain.
