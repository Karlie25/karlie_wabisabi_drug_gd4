How much does the service cost?
The service cost may vary due to the number of sessions and special conditions, and customers should contact the clinic for more information.
Does the treatment have any side effects?
Customers will notice some side effects they may experience after each section, such as dizziness, vomits, loss of balance, sensitivity to light, and mood swings. Note that all symptoms are temporary and will disappear within a day. However, if the side effects worsen, contacting the clinic is best.
